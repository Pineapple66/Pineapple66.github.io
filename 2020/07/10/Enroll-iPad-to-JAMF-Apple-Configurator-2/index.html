Enroll iPad to JAMF - Apple Configurator 2

Posted on 2020-07-10 Edited on 2021-03-09 In System Administration

pple Configurator 2 is a free macOS tool that allows you manage all of the iOS devices. In the post-pandemic world, there are numbers of students who have a need to take classes by using iPads. Apple configurator 2 comes handy when enroll them to Jamf. This guide will walk you through how to configure them.

Task: Install management software so we can keep them up to date.

Prerequisite: An active Jamf management portal.

Step 1 Install Apple Configurator 2

locate and install the newest Apple configurator 2 from the Apple store. Update your MAC OS before perform this action.

Step 2 Import Organizations

Launch the program, on the top left corner select Apple Configurator2, and then select Preferences

Import Organizations. Click “+” on the bottom left to add a new organization, hit “Next” to continue

Login in with your Apple School account. If you are not the admin; please communicate with Terry or Marjory for obtaining the administrator rights on your account.

Select “Generate a new supervision identity.”, and hit “Done”.
As a side note, you may need to enter your local computer username and password to permit this action.

At this point, You have successfully add your Organizations.

Step 3 Add MDM servers

Similar to adding Organizations, Click bottom left “+” icon to add a new MDM server

Enter the name, and the MDM server URL, which located in your JAMF school portal

Enter the name, and the MDM server URL, which located in your JAMF school portal

Click “Next” when you finish.

Review the information that you just entered. Make sure *.jamfcloud.com is selected, and then click save.

The Enrollment URL may changed during the setup, but it does not affect the normal enrollment.

At this point, You have successfully add your MDM server.

Step 4 Create a Blueprint

Select “File” on the top left corner, then select “New Blueprint”

“Untitled” Blueprint pop-up and you may re-name it.

Step 5 Specify the MDM server and Organization for this Blueprint

With this blue print select, click the Prepare button on the top

Select Prepare with “manual configuration”, check “Supervise devices”, and “allow device to pair with other computers”.

Select “xxMDM” (This is the MDM server that you specify earlier) as the MDM management server, click next when you done.

Select the correct Organization, click next when you are done.

Blueprint is ready!

Step 6 Prepare the device with the blueprint

Use the lighting or USB Type-C to USB cable connect IPad to the iMac.

With the IPad select (single click), Click actions on the top left of the Apple configurator, then select Apply with the blueprint that we just created.

Hit “Apply” when you see the warning pop-up.

If this iPad has been already configure/ supervised before, you may receive this pop-up, hit “Erase” to continue with this setup.

Step 7 Connect to Wifi

Go to iPad itself and connect it to the Wifi

Step 7 You are done!

You should see this iPad from the JAMF management portal, and you can take over the management from the web.

** As long as the management files stays and connected to the network, you will have the ability to configure this device remotely.**
